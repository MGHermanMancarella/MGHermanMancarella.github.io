In August of 2022 I started my journey to tech by self learning JavaScript. Four months later I was accepted into Rithm School’s 34th Cohort and I feel so lucky to have the opportunity.

I am very mechanically inclined. I’ve always had a healthy curiosity of how and why things work. I love working on cars, appliances, and electronics because they’re each a symphony of parts playing one song.

Prior to my foray into coding, I spent fourteen years working at Trader Joe’s in the San Francisco Bay Area. I learned to look at each part of the store, every detail, and how to see how those parts fit into the store as a whole.

Now, just like the grocery store, when I look at a website or use an app I can see how each part fits into the bigger picture. I love being able to look at source code and decipher what each piece is doing – to see the symphony in action.
